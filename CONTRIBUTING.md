# Contributing to Repo Checklists

Contributions are welcome when they improve checklist clarity, usefulness, or coverage.

## Good Contributions

- Sharper checklist wording.
- Better checklist structure.
- New checklist types that are broadly useful.
- Fixes for inconsistency or ambiguity.

## Checklist Principles

A good checklist should be clear, practical, concise, reusable and aligned with [Repo Standards](https://github.com/brandonhimpfen/repo-standards).

## Before Submitting

Please make sure proposed additions are not overly specific to one organization or workflow unless clearly labeled as examples or optional patterns.
